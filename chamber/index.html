Our Downtown

Bellevue is a city in Washington state. It's in the Seattle metropolitan area, known for its shopping centers, including the large upscale Bellevue Square. The Bellevue Arts Museum exhibits crafts and design. The Bellevue Botanical Garden highlights Pacific Northwest plants, and includes a wetlands area. Nearby, the KidsQuest Children’s Museum features interactive science, tech and art exhibits. Downtown Park has a large lawn and a waterfall.
