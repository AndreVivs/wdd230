Demographics and Statistical Data:

Population: As of 2022 estimates, Bellevue has a population of approximately 150,606 residents.

Median Age: The median age of residents is 38.3 years.

Ethnic Diversity: Bellevue is diverse, with 44.1% White (Non-Hispanic) and 39.4% Asian (Non-Hispanic) residents.

Languages Spoken: In addition to English, the most spoken languages at home include Chinese, Spanish, Telugu, Russian, and Hindi.

Events and Attractions!

Major Annual Events

Bellevue Arts Fair: Held in the last week of July, this fair showcases art and crafts.

Bellevue Fashion Week: Takes place in late September, highlighting the latest fashion trends.

Bellevue Magic Season: Throughout December, the city is decorated with holiday lights, ice skating, and seasonal exhibits.
